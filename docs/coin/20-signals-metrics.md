# Signals and Metrics (COIN / Stabilization)

## Signal categories (aggregate)
1) Population security: harm reports, violence trends, intimidation indicators.
2) Governance performance: service uptime, response latency, complaint resolution throughput.
3) Economic stability: market function proxies, outage impact, displacement stress.
4) Information environment: rumor volatility, narrative shocks, information voids.
5) Civil dispute channels: access, throughput, perceived fairness, corruption proxies.

## Measurement principles
- Prefer effect metrics tied to lived outcomes over output counts.
- Use denominators (per-capita / per-household / per-route-mile).
- Track baselines and deltas; avoid “single-number” dashboards without context.
- Explicitly document uncertainty, missingness, and likely confounders.

## Fog-of-war integration
Every metric has:
- freshness / staleness
- confidence / error bounds
- coverage notes (what is not observed)
- refresh cadence (how often we must re-scout)
