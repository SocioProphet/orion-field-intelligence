# COIN Decision Cards (Extension)

COIN decision cards are effects-first operational plans. They recommend multi-channel next actions and define how to measure whether the actions improved legitimacy and stability.

## Required sections
- Situation summary (what is happening)
- Legitimacy state (aggregate)
- Grievance drivers (hypotheses)
- Constraints (ROE/policy, logistics, comms, time)
- Assumptions (explicit, falsifiable)
- Recommended actions (multi-channel)
- Expected effects (on indicators)
- Measurement plan (how we confirm)
- Refresh/scouting tasks (what to re-observe, by when)
- Fog-of-war status (staleness, gaps, contradiction)
